500 Word Reflection:

Growing additions are to be included in this portfolio. In the meantime, there is this logo. This is a binary-pixel art piece of graphic design I decidedly referred to as Aquamarine Lovewatch. It is at it's core, a gradient including colors from aquamarine to teal, then black. Amidst the center of the art is a simple clock, wherein it tells the time and serves as measurement for the passing of time in this abstract place of vast color. To me, the logo represents the passing of time and the constant change that it entails. To explain how I truly made the design, I'll have to step back a little. Visualize what happened and type words that are pleasing to my eyes and mind. So far they are ugly, so I have to paste and edit an excerpt of a story I made some time ago, it's related to this.
The calmest rain gently tapped against the window, casual patters of water droplets lightly knocking on the window in front of him.
